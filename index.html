About the Event

A collaborative workshop organized by MoroccoAI and Meta to integrate global and local insights on “AI Policies Between Innovation and Regulation” and designed to bridge global expertise with local perspectives on AI governance. This exclusive event brings together policymakers, industry leaders, and academic experts to shape the future of AI regulation in an inclusive and innovative way.

This workshop represents a pivotal opportunity to influence the trajectory of AI governance in emerging markets while establishing Morocco as a regional leader in thoughtful AI policy development.

Collaborative Platform

A unique forum bringing together experts, regional policymakers, and local innovators to develop contextually relevant AI governance frameworks that balance innovation with responsible deployment.

Knowledge Exchange

Interactive sessions designed to share insights from established AI governance models while incorporating emerging market perspectives and regional priorities specific to North Africa and the broader MENA region.

Policy Development

Working groups focused on drafting actionable policy recommendations that address both global standards and local implementation challenges, with emphasis on creating inclusive AI development pathways.
